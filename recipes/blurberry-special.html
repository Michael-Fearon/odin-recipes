About The Recipe

The Blurberry special can be mixed with 1 vodka, 1 brandy, 1 gin, 1 orange, and 2 lemons. It can be finished by adding 1 equa leaf, 1 sliced lime, 1 diced lemon, and 1 diced orange as a garnish. This requires 37 Cooking to make.
It heals 7 Hitpoints, increases Strength by 5% + 2 levels, and reduces Attack by 2% - 3 levels.

All of these ingredients can be purchased at Funch's Fine Groceries and cost a total of 30 coins per Blurberry special made (not including cocktail shaker, cocktail glass, or knife).

Steps

  1. Pour 1 jigger of vodka, brandy and gin into your cocktail shaker
  2. Juice an orange and two lemons, saving some lemon and orange peel for garnish
  3. Add orange and lemon juice to the cocktail shaker, along with a scoop of ice
  4. Thoroughly shake (not stir) to combine
  5. Pour into cocktail glass and garnish with equa leaf, sliced lime and the lemon & orange peels from earlier
