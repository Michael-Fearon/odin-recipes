About The Recipe

A tangled toad's legs is a food that players consume in one bite, healing 15 hitpoints.
Players may need to make one as part of Training part I for the Gnome Restaurant minigame.

Members can make a tangled toad's legs via gnome cooking with 40 Cooking, yielding 185 experience. Like all gnome foods, they stop burning at Cooking level 38.

Gianne dough, gnomebowl moulds, cheese, equa leaves, dwellberries, and gnome spice can be bought from the Gnome Cook named Hudo who is located in the north-west corner the 1st floor[UK] of the Grand Tree.
While toad's legs are not sold, swamp toads may be found in the swamp north-west of the Grand Tree, in the Tree Gnome Stronghold.

Steps

  1. Use a gianne dough on a gnomebowl mould to make a raw gnomebowl
  2. Cook the raw gnomebowl on a range to make half baked bowl
  3. Prepare a half made bowl (tangled toad's legs) with 4 toad's legs, 2 cheese, 2 equa leaves, 1 gnome spice, and 1 dwellberry
  4. Cook the half made bowl (tangled toad's legs) on a range to make tangled toad's legs
