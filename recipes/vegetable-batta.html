About The Recipe

A vegetable batta is a food that players consume in one bite, healing 11 hitpoints.
Players may need to make one as part of Training part I for the Gnome Restaurant minigame.

Members can make a vegetable batta via gnome cooking with 30 Cooking, yielding 156 experience. Like all gnome foods, they stop burning at Cooking level 38.

Gianne dough, batta tins, tomatoes, dwellberries, onion, cheese, cabbage, and equa leaves can be bought from the Gnome Cook named Hudo who is located in the north-west corner the 1st floor[UK] of the Grand Tree.

Steps

  1. Use a gianne dough on a batta tin to make a raw batta
  2. Cook the raw batta on a range to make half baked batta
  3. Prepare a half made batta (vegetable) with 2 tomatoes, 1 dwellberries, 1 onion, 1 cheese, and 1 cabbage
  4. Cook the half made batta (vegetable) on a range to make unfinished batta (vegetable)
  5. Use 1 equa leaves on the unfinished batta (vegetable) to finish a vegetable batta
